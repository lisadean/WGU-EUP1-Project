Computer and Mobile Games

Borderlands 2 is a first person shooter and action roleplaying computer game. You play as a Vault Hunter looking for treasure who wind up helping a resistance movement on the planet to fight against a villian known as Handsome Jack. Various alien monsters are encountered along the way alongs with guns. Lots and lots of guns. Co-operative gameplay the the characters' hilarious dialogue make for a very fun game.

Everquest is the first massively multiplayer online game I was exposed to. I spent many hours adventuring in this online fantasy world and met many friends both online and in real life through this game.
