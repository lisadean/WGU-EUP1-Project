Twenty Questions

Twenty Questions is one of the most basic of games. One person picks an object (or whatever category is agreed upon) and the other people have twenty questions to try to figure out what it is.

I Spy

Very similar to Twenty Questions is I Spy. This game is usually played played with kids to distract them from enforced boredom caused by travel or waiting. One person chooses an object they can see and says "I spy with my little eye something that begins with 'M'" or "I spy with my little eye something living" or some small description to get the game going. Then the other people try to guess what the first person saw.
